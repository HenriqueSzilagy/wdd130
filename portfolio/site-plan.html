Home page

Hi Employer, Tech Recruiter or just curious, welcome to my personal website where you will get to know more about me and my Portfolio, This website was made with my personal skills as part of an activity of my studies at Brigham Young University Idaho , So I took the opportunity to expose my portfolio to you. In the About Me section, you will have the opportunity to find out more about my hobbies, age, where I live, professional skills (curriculum), courses I studied, my education, my goals and what I intend to study in the future! In the Portfolio section you will have the opportunity to see my Personal Projects such as Dashboard and Reports produced with Power Bi tools At the bottom of the site, you will see my social networks and my ways to contact me, I am always available to schedule a conversation or interview to exchange experiences and talk about job opportunities.
